Account controls

Delete your account

Delete directly in Jigsaw Puzzle Games, or create an email request if you no longer have access to the app.

Fastest: delete inside the app

  1. Open Jigsaw Puzzle Games and select Settings.
  2. Select Account and sign in if necessary.
  3. Select Delete account, review the warning, and confirm.

The signed-in app account and its synchronized cloud progress are permanently deleted. The app signs you out when deletion succeeds.

What is not deleted automatically Local guest progress already stored on your device remains available. Reset it in Settings, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. Deleting this app account does not delete your Google or Apple account.

Request deletion without the app

Complete the details below. Selecting the button opens your email application with a deletion request addressed to our support team. You must review and send the email to submit the request.

This webpage does not transmit or store the information you type. Your email application sends it only after you review and send the prepared message. You may also contact directly with the subject "Jigsaw Puzzle Games account deletion request".
