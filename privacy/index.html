Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

This policy explains how Jigsaw Puzzle Games ("the app", "we", or "us") handles information when you use the app. The game can be played without creating an account.

1. Information handled by the app

Guest play and local progress

Puzzle progress, settings, saved boards, hint balances, and achievements are stored locally on your device. This information is not uploaded to our cloud service unless you choose to sign in and enable progress synchronization.

Optional account and cloud sync

If you sign in with email, Google, or Apple, our authentication provider, Supabase, creates an app account. We may process your email address, app user ID, limited profile information supplied by the sign-in provider, authentication records, and synchronized game progress. We use this information to authenticate you, back up progress, and make it available across supported devices.

Purchases

Lifetime Premium purchases are processed by Google Play or Apple, and entitlement status is managed through RevenueCat. We receive purchase and entitlement information such as product, transaction status, store, app user ID, and purchase dates. We do not receive or store your full payment-card details.

Advertising

Non-Premium users may see banner ads and may choose to watch a rewarded ad for a hint. Google AdMob and its partners may process device identifiers, IP-derived information, ad interactions, diagnostics, and related technical data to deliver, secure, measure, and prevent fraud in advertising. The app requests non-personalized ads and uses Google's consent tools where required. Premium users do not receive ads after Premium status is confirmed.

2. How information is used

  • Provide gameplay, save progress, and synchronize optional cloud data.
  • Authenticate accounts and maintain account security.
  • Process, restore, and verify Lifetime Premium access.
  • Provide ads and rewarded hints to eligible non-Premium users.
  • Diagnose reliability issues, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

3. Service providers

We use Supabase for authentication and cloud progress, RevenueCat for purchase entitlement management, Google Play and Apple for store services and payments, and Google AdMob for advertising and consent management. These providers process information under their own privacy policies and contractual terms.

4. Sharing and sale

We share information with the providers above only as needed to operate their respective services, comply with law, or protect users and the app. We do not sell personal information for money. Advertising-related processing may be treated differently under some regional privacy laws; use the in-app advertising privacy controls where they are available.

5. Retention and deletion

Local guest progress remains on your device until you reset it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app. Cloud account data is retained while the account is active and is deleted when an account-deletion request is completed, subject to limited records that a provider must retain for security, fraud prevention, tax, accounting, transaction, or other legal obligations.

Deleting the Jigsaw Puzzle Games account does not delete your Google account, Apple account, or store purchase history. See our account deletion page for instructions.

6. Your choices

  • Play entirely as a guest without creating an app account.
  • Sign out or delete a signed-in account from the app's Account screen.
  • Review available advertising choices from Settings in the app.
  • Reset local game progress from Settings.
  • Request deletion through our external deletion page.

7. Security and international processing

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted network connections and access controls. No system is completely secure. Our providers may process information in countries other than your own, subject to their safeguards and applicable law.

8. Children

The app is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from a child under 13. Contact us if you believe a child has provided account information so that we can investigate and delete it.

9. Changes

We may update this policy when the app or applicable requirements change. We will update the effective date on this page and provide additional notice when required.
